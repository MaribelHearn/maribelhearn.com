Fill in the best survivals you have pulled off in the table below. If you leave a dropdown menu on the N/A option, it will not be factored in. At the bottom of the page, three different sortable tables indicating your survival progress will be generated.

NM = No Miss (no deaths), NB = No Bomb, NN = No Miss No Bomb, NNN = NN with a third restriction depending on the game. In PCB this restriction is No Border Breaks, in UFO it is No UFO Summons and in TD it is No Trance. The Phantasm Stage counts as another Extra Stage.
