Touhou Tier List Creator

Notice: this page is not designed for mobile use. Apologies for the inconvenience.

How to use:

  • Click a character and then a tier to add that character to it.
  • While holding a character, right click to put them back.
  • Right click a character in a tier to remove them from that tier.
  • Click a tier and then another tier to swap their positions.
  • Right click a tier to remove that tier and all of its contents.
