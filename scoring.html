Scoring Calculator


Enter your high scores and which shottypes you used to achieve them. You can leave any high score empty.
The scores will be compared to the world records that were achieved with the same shottypes and percentages will be given.
At the bottom of the page, a table will be printed that ranks your scores by their percentages of the world records.
Your scores should not include any characters other than digits, dots, commas and spaces.

World records are current as of September 27, 2017.
